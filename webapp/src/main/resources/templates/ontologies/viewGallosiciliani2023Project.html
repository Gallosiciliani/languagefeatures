The following OWL vocabularies and datasets have been developed in the scope of this project.

The whole production workflow for the ontology of Nouns and Verbs of the Gallo-Italic variety spoken in Nicosia and Sperlinga, aside with all the software tools and intermediate products, is available in the workflow page.

In addition, the Gallo-Siciliani github repository provide all the source codes of these tools.

Finally, the scientific works funded by the project are the followings:

  • The Strange Case of the Gallo-Italic Dialects of Sicily: Preservation and Innovation in Contact-Induced Change
  • Sulla creatività linguistica della perifrasi ‘mostrare a’ + infinito nel dialetto galloitalico di Nicosia
  • Contact-induced change in Sicilian Gallo-Italic. The multipurpose Aviri a + Infinitive Construction in the dialect of Nicosia
  • An OWL Ontology for Linguistic Phenomena with Applications to Gallo-Italic Dialects in Sicily
