Syntactic questionnaires

The syntactic questionnaires contained in this section serve to test the influence of Sicilian syntax on the grammatical system of Gallo-Italic varieties. The phenomena investigated are as follows: Differential Object Marking (DOM), the rise of clitic pronouns and pseudo-coordination. Added to these are the so-called loss of the infinitive, a phenomenon limited to the north-eastern triangle of Sicily, and the two-complementary system, remnants of which are partially preserved only in Sperlinghese. Finally, the investigation of personal pronouns explores the causes of syncretism, whereby in Sanfratellano the 1st and 2nd person singular pronouns, and in Nicosiano/Sperlinghese only the 2nd person singular pronoun, also perform the functions of direct, indirect and oblique objects.
